How to beat the Lady Dukes

Opposing coach scouting report · WPA Lady Dukes 10U · 74 games · 2025–26

Team profile
🚨 Respect these
Top-4 offense is elite. Porter (.496), LaFever (.424), Glomb (.401), Dantry (.407) — all above .400 BA. Do not let these four beat you.
Team speed is extreme. 414 SBs. LaFever steals 1.2/game. Glomb steals 1.0/game. If they get on, they're going.
Three pitchers also hit. Porter, Glomb, and Dantry don't rest in the field — they're your 3 best hitters.
+138 run differential. When this team gets momentum, runs come in bunches. Don't let them string innings.
✅ Exploit these
Close-game record: 9-17. They lose 1 and 2-run games at a 65% rate. Make it a grind.
Bottom half K rate 45–79%. Spots 6–9 go down swinging constantly. Attack them, don't nibble.
Run on their catchers. 14% caught-stealing rate. Run every time you're on — they cannot throw you out.
Away from home, offense drops. Top hitters hit .080–.120 lower on the road. This is a home-field team.
Rocco walks batters (3.22/inn). When she pitches, take pitches — she'll give you the base.
Game plan
Pitching strategy
Keep the game close. Limit extra base hits against the top 4. Pitch around Porter if you have to. Attack spots 6–9 aggressively — they will swing and miss. Work up in the zone to Tortorice (extreme groundball hitter).
Baserunning strategy
Run on every pitch. Both catchers throw out fewer than 15% of runners — this is free bases. Set up steals in scoring position early to put pressure on before the big inning.
Defensive setup
Play up the middle — both CF and 2B see heavy traffic from the groundball-heavy offense (team GB% ~55%). Shift Schmidt at SS — she's an extreme pull hitter with low contact. Shade Tortorice to pull (69% GB).
