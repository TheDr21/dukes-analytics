Team Goals

▾
50–70%

Target win rate while consistently pushing players beyond their comfort zones.

We will not play in lesser tournaments to chase trophies. Competition level is chosen intentionally.

🏛️

Core Development Pillars

▾
01

Fundamentals First

Throwing, catching, hitting mechanics, fielding, and baserunning as the foundation for long-term success.

02

Game IQ & Ownership

Situational awareness, decision-making, and leadership. Catchers call pitches and manage game strategy.

03

Mental Performance

Growth mindset, focus under pressure, confidence, and resilience. Effort and attitude are non-negotiable.

04

Team-First Culture

Accountability and a unified environment centered on effort, attitude, and support — parents included.

05

Competitive Progression

Intentional higher-level competition to prepare players for the next stage of their growth.

📈

Player Development Approach

▾
  • Team-wide emphasis on hitting approach and overall game strategy — every player builds situational play awareness.
  • Specialty skill development (pitching, catching) through small group and individualized sessions led by Coach Steve.
  • Pitcher workload management: targeting approximately six innings per day or twelve innings per tournament.
  • Depth building across the pitching staff is a priority.
  • Individual development areas identified and refined throughout practices for each athlete.
📅

Proposed Season Schedule

▾
Fall 2026

Fall Season

  • Participation in up to four tournaments, focusing on scrimmage-style play and adjusting to increased pitching distance and ball size.
  • Hosted events at Highlands Sports Complex (Triadelphia, WV) and McDonald, PA.
  • Two practices per week through November (adjusted based on final scrimmage schedule).
  • Mandatory Thursday night hitting sessions with Brian Barca (make-ups at parent expense).
Winter 2026

Off-Season Development

  • Continued mandatory weekly hitting sessions with Brian Barca.
  • Focus on mental approach, timing, preparation, and overall player health.
  • Supplemental indoor practices added January through March.
Spring / Summer 2027

Spring & Summer Season

  • Training sessions with Brian Barca — attendance is mandatory.
  • Participation in approximately 6–10 tournaments.
  • At least two premier tournaments to elevate level of play and experience.
  • Primary focus: 11U A/B competition; selective participation in 12U C events.
  • Supplemental indoor practices as needed based on weather and development priorities.
