Scouting Notes
⚠ Most Complete Team Scouted
No obvious weakness. Elite pitching, relentless lineup, two-way players throughout. You need a near-perfect game to beat this team. Every run will be earned.
Peyton S — Walk Her
.487 AVG is the highest of any player scouted at the 10U level. With runners on base, consider pitching around her. She will not make outs under pressure. Do not give her a pitch to hit.
Survive Amelia H
310 Ks in 147 innings — you will strike out. The key is limiting free passes, making contact when you can, and keeping games close enough to win late. Work deep counts to raise her pitch count.
Attack Toni M or Gracie K
If Amelia H or Skylar R comes out, the ERA jumps dramatically (3.68 and 4.70). Manufacture runs, put pressure on the defense, and capitalize when the secondary arms enter.
Bottom Order Has Holes
Kitty S (67 K) and Cora D (47 K) both have high strikeout totals despite decent OBP from walks. Attack them early in counts with strikes and generate outs quickly in the bottom third.
Amelia H — Walks Are High
79 BB in 147 IP is 0.54 per inning. Despite the elite ERA, she gives up walks. Patient hitters who take pitches will get on base via walk. Don't expand the zone — make her find it.
