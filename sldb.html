Weighting every way on base

wOBA

wOBA by player

Dashed line is the team mark. wOBA sits on the on-base scale, so think of it the way you read OBP — higher is better, and the gap from the line is value created per trip.

The leaderboard

Index puts her wOBA against the team's: 100 is exactly team-average, 130 means 30% better per plate appearance.
